Welcome! Here's a guide to getting started with YourVoice as a user

After installing the software, follow the steps below.

Step 1 – Create an Account

Click on the create an account button on the main page that takes you to the main page for registration.


NOTE: If you where invited with a link register with the email that was used to send you the invite.

Step 2 – Enter your details

This is where you enter your details and verify your identity that you need access to the platform.


NOTE: The account details would be shown below for yoiu to verify the sign in details.


NOTE: The account shown here is the account that your vote would be casted in.

To verify your details view the verification and check the details there, click here.

Step 3 – Enter Election ID

Once we have gotten to the dashboard click on the election id button.

Click the ‘add election’ button.

Select the account you want to vote for when you have entered the election ID.

Accept the condition on the modal, then proceed to the election and click on the person you want to vote for:

Click here to install your ad accounts now in another tab.

Step 4 – Cast your vote

Want to shorten the links you publish? Click ‘General’ within the settings wheel to connect your bit.ly accounts to your social profiles.

Click here to install your bit.ly account now in another tab.

Step 5 – Submit your Ballot!

You are allowed to retake your votes if you feel you have made a mistake, when you are sure of your selection you are allow to submit your ballot.

Once your Ballot has been submitted You can:

  • View the live Election Result.
  • View the leaderboard of the election
  • View other statistics of the election.
  • Read our FAQ.

Click here to explore all your profile settings in another tab.

Step 6 – View your live Result!

After your ballot has been submitted you can.

View the live election result:

  • in a grid manner.
  • in a list manner.
  • with the amount of votes each contestants has gotten.

This page would be updated at real time as long as the election is ongoing.
