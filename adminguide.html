Welcome! Here's a guide to getting started with YourVoice as an Administrator

You’re just six easy steps from being fully set up. Feel free to open YourVoice in another tab and follow along.

Step 1 – Create an Account as an Administrator

Click on your organization in the upper click here left corner to add more Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn or YouTube profiles.


NOTE: If the “+” sign is grayed out then you’ll need to ask a teammate with Agorapulse Manager permissions to add them for you.

Step 2 – Log into the Platform

This is also where you’ll invite more teammates onboard to craft content and manage your messages together.


NOTE: If this option is grayed out then you’ll need to ask a teammate with Agorapulse Manager permissions to add them for you.

To see a full list of actions each Agorapulse role can and cannot perform, click here.

Step 3 – Verify your Details

Once we’ve connected your ad accounts, your team will be able to manage the comments on your social ads.

Click the ‘settings’ wheel.

Select a Facebook or Instagram profile, then connect one or more ad accounts.

Rinse, lather and repeat to install ad accounts on other profiles and voila! Your inbox will now collect the comments on your Facebook and Instagram ads. You can use Inbox filters to manage ad comments only, here:

To create a new Election:

  1. Enter Election Name
  2. Enter Election Start and end Date and Time
  3. Enter Election Logo

Click here to install your ad accounts now in another tab.

Step 4 – Proceed to Admin Dashboard

Want to shorten the links you publish? Click ‘General’ within the settings wheel to connect your bit.ly accounts to your social profiles.

Click here to install your bit.ly account now in another tab.

Step 5 – Add Position

You can rename and rearrange your profiles or group them together.  Click Menu/groups from your Organization Settings to re-order or rename your profiles.

To group your profiles together:

  1. Click create new group
  2. Title your group
  3. Slide the appropriate profiles into your new group
